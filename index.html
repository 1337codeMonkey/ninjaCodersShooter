WELCOME

TehShootie is a classic 2-D shmup for the android platform.

This game is being built with the ambition of bringing back old skool shoot'em ups (shmup) back to the masses. We hope to invoke both nostalgia and joy into the hearts of our players by not only reviving the shmup platform, but by modernizing it with new and fun features. Follow us on the social network of your choice to keep up with the progress of our development.
